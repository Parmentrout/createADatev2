how it works

Remember those choose your own adventure books from when you were a kid? We modeled "create a date" off of those fond memories! You start by creating a date and then fill in activity choices based on your location. When you go on your date your significant other will be able to choose between the location and then subsequent activities. It will be an experience you'll never forget. Still confused?
